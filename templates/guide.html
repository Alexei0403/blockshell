üÜï Initialize New Blockchain

First we will initialize the new blockchain with following command in blockshell CLI.

Above command initializes new blockchain and creates chain.txt in your working directory where our blockchain data will be stored. You can compare this chain.txt file as real world blockchain‚Äôs ledger.

‚Äìdifficulty number indicates the difficulty level for blockchain‚Äôs proof of work. More the number, more time will be taken to mine new block in our blockchain.

In our blockchain we have Initial Zeros Proof of Work algorithm. So, when we want to mine new block, our PoW will look for the hash that has the initial 3 characters are zeros(0) as right now our blockchain‚Äôs difficulty level is set to 3 at the time of initialization.

Let‚Äôs store some data and Mine very first block of our blockchain by doing PoW.

Blockshell comes with build-in command dotx which is used to create a new transaction and this will mine new block with given data (eg. hello blockchain)

Output

Mining this block will depend on the difficulty level of the blockchain. Right now difficulty is of 3 and thats why our block mined in less than a second. After mining action, we received hash of that block with 3 initials as zero as difficulty is 3.
